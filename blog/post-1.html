Launching Clutch Click AI

January 27, 2026

I've been building startups since 2018, and I've seen the same problem over and over: businesses struggle to get visible online. Traditional SEO is expensive and complicated. Most companies either overpay for mediocre results or give up entirely.

Then AI search engines started showing up everywhere. ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity—people aren't just searching on Google anymore. And I realized businesses needed help navigating this new landscape.

That's why I built Clutch Click AI. It's an AI visibility platform that helps businesses show up wherever their customers are searching—whether that's Google, ChatGPT, or anywhere else.

What We Built

We built two things, really. A platform you can use yourself to track and optimize your AI visibility. And an agency team that does the work for you if you'd rather focus on running your business.

Both work. It's flexible. Some people want control, others want someone else to handle it. We built it so you can choose what makes sense for your business.

Why This Matters

Organic visibility shouldn't be something only big companies with huge budgets can afford. Too many businesses are getting left behind because SEO feels impossible or too expensive.

We're trying to change that. Whether you use the platform yourself or have our team manage everything, the goal is the same: help your business grow organically through SEO, AI SEO, and AEO (AI Engine Optimization).

It's been months in the making. We listened to what people actually needed—tools that work, flexibility in how they engage with us, and results that scale with their business.

I'm excited about what we've built and what we can help businesses achieve. If you're trying to get visible online, whether you're a startup or a larger company, we built this for you.

Check it out at clutchclick.com.
